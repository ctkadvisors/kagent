| ------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `SnapshotCache` → projection   | Cache is populated by the operator informer; projection reads it without auth (in-process). Untrusted data appearing in cache (via tampered AgentTask) flows directly into the response.                                                            |
